Erin Sturm

President

My favorite thing to do is strategize! If you don't know where to start with your social media presence, I'd love to help you create a brand, a message, and a plan. If you know what you want to do, but don't have the time or energy to execute your plan, I can help you there too! I create original content and curate the best of what the internet has to offer.

I love learning and am obsessed with sharing knowledge with others. Knowledge is power!

Prior to beginning a freelance career, I spent six years working as an Information Specialist in a corporate library at a biotechnology company. I use the skills from my previous life to research my industry and my clients' industries. I also search for, and plan imaginary road trips to, restaurants that left town when I was a child - I miss you, Olga's Kitchen!

I love reading and am always looking for book recommendations so tweet me @aperfectsturm2 if you have a suggestion! If you're interested in freelancing and motherhood, check out my blog, Freelancing Mama.

My greatest joy is my daughter, but chocolate is a close second. 

Contact me at erinmsturm@gmail.com
